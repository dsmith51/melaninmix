Our Mission


We are “The Melanin Mix”, a podcast that is available on all streaming platforms. The Melanin Mix is a chatcast that covers a variety of topics that aims to give black women and minorities a safe space to voice their unique experiences. We also offer our perspectives and life experiences on the show and we bring on a diverse set of guests to share their opinions and experiences on topics that are relevant for our listeners. We are known for our ability to have well-structured yet entertaining conversations on relevant topics in relation to our community. We are most proud when we hear that the conversations on our show have positively impacted our listeners.
